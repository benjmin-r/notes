Gundo Cheatsheet
================

Configuration
-------------

::

    map <silent> <leader>u :GundoToggle<CR>

Command
-------

+-------+----------------------------------+
| gg    | head                             |
+-------+----------------------------------+
| G     | tail                             |
+-------+----------------------------------+
| Enter | revert changes                   |
+-------+----------------------------------+
| p     | show diff                        |
+-------+----------------------------------+
| P     | show diff movie and make changes |
+-------+----------------------------------+
